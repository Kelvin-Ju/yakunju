Yakun Ju (舉雅琨)

I am now a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Department of Electronic and Information Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, working with Prof. Kin-Man Lam.

I earned my Ph.D. at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Ocean University of China, supervised by Prof.Junyu Dong, in June 2022 (successive master-doctor program). Before that, I earned my B.Sc. at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Sichuan University, in June 2016.

I'm also grateful for having collaborated with these inspiring professors: Boxin Shi (PKU), Huiyu Zhou (UL), Sheng Chen (Soton), Wuyuan Xie (SZU), etc.

My research interests focus on Photometric Stereo, Image Processing, Low-level Computer Vision, Computational Photography, and Machine Learning. If you want to cooperate with me, kindly contact me via E-mail.
